About Us

Every day, life throws countless trivial choices at us and simultaneously tons of decisions for us to make.

What do people do every day? They eat food.

Our mission is to help people save time every day by making some choices for them. As college students ourselves, we hear or say every day, "Where do you wanna go to eat?" which is followed by a waste of about 30 minutes, deciding.

Even if you are not in a group, you may be travelling or just looking for a new and interesting place to eat. We are here to help.

Our tool only picks from the best of the best just so you can rest assured that our restaurant picker will help you find your next favorite spot to eat in no time!

Just enter your zip code, your preferences (if any), and hit Go!
